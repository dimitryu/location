Privacy Policy

Family Location  ·  com.dimitryu.familylocation

Effective date: May 16, 2026  |  Last updated: May 16, 2026

Family Location is a real-time GPS tracking app designed for families. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used and protected.

1. Who We Are

Family Location is developed and maintained by Dmitry Ushka (dimitryu@gmail.com). If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at the email above.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the family location-sharing service:

  • Precise GPS location – collected in the foreground and background while the app is active, to display your real-time position to family members in your group.
  • Account information – email address and display name provided during sign-in (via Firebase Authentication).
  • Device information – device model and operating system version, used for diagnostics only.
  • Location history – a limited log of recent location updates stored per user to allow viewing recent movement. This log is automatically pruned.

We do not collect payment information, contacts, photos, microphone data, or any data unrelated to the service.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your real-time location on the family map visible to members of your group.
  • To send push notifications to group members (e.g., SOS alerts, arrival/departure events).
  • To maintain your account and group membership.
  • To improve app stability and diagnose crashes (anonymised).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Background Location

Family Location requests the "Always On" (background) location permission because its core feature — sharing your live position with your family — requires location updates even when the app is not actively open on screen.

Location updates are transmitted only to the Firebase Firestore database linked to your group. No other party receives your location data. You can revoke background location access at any time in your device's Settings → Apps → Family Location → Permissions.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore & Authentication), hosted in secure Google Cloud infrastructure.
  • Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS/TLS connections.
  • Access to location data is restricted by Firestore security rules: only authenticated members of your group can read your location.
  • We do not store data on our own servers.

6. Data Retention

  • Live location is overwritten with each update (we store only the latest position).
  • Location history logs are retained for a limited rolling period and are automatically deleted.
  • Account data is retained for as long as your account exists. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 8).

7. Children's Privacy

Family Location is designed for use by families including minors. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. Because this app is intended to be set up and managed by a parent or guardian, the parent is responsible for granting necessary permissions on a child's device.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13 without consent, please contact us at dimitryu@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your account and all associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for background location by changing device permissions.

To request account deletion or a copy of your data, email us at dimitryu@gmail.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

9. Third-Party Services

Family Location uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase – authentication, database, and push notifications.
  • Google Maps (react-native-maps) – map rendering on Android.
  • Expo – app build and update infrastructure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify users via an in-app message. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
